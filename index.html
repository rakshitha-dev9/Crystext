What is CrysText

Crystal prediction, reimagined with language

Traditional crystal structure prediction requires expensive DFT calculations and deep domain expertise. CrysText replaces that with a single natural language prompt.

01 ·
Text-conditioned generation
Describe any material in plain English. The model generates lattice parameters, space group, and atom coordinates automatically.
02 ·
Fine-tuned Mistral-7B
Built on Mistral-7B-v0.3 with QLoRA (r=16), trained on the full MP-20 benchmark of 27,136 real crystal structures.
03 ·
Standard CIF output
Outputs industry-standard CIF files directly importable into VESTA, Materials Studio, or pymatgen.
04 ·
Materials informatics
Part of the push to accelerate materials discovery using AI — faster and more accessible than classical DFT methods.
